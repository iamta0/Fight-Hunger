INTRODUCTION

Food Security remains central to developmental issues across the global landscape. This is reflected in the Goal 2 of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) which addresses zero hunger. It entails the emplacements of implementation measures to ensure that everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food towards ending poverty, promoting health and fostering sustainable development.

Nigeria has experienced its own unpleasant share of food insecurity thus, forming a foundational framework for actualizing President Bola Tinubu's RENEWED HOPE AGENDA is the Enthronement of Food Security. To this end, the administration has deployed effective mechanisms to address the challenges of hunger, thus birthing the positive results currently being experienced across the country. In alignment with the administration's agenda, FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is put in place to bolster and complement the administration's goals accordingly.

THEMATIC AREAS

FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is an initiative constructed around three interconnected thematic focus areas of Empowerment, Advocacy, and Exhibition, all designed as catalyst for sustainable growth and development. Each focus area is strategically targeted at creating solutions for hunger reduction that can be scaled and integrated across the nation's diverse population, forming a tripod towards advancing the RENEWED HOPE AGENDA on Food Security in Nigeria.

EMPOWERMENT

The empowerment model of the FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA project forms a central pillar of the initiative. It focuses on equipping communities with the tools, skills, and resources necessary to become self-sufficient, improve their livelihoods, and sustainably address hunger. By focusing on education and access to resources, the program ensures that people are not only surviving hunger but are also empowered to overcome it in the long run. This approach fosters resilience, builds stronger communities, and leads to lasting changes that can transform Nigeria's food security landscape. The focus areas are as follows

ACCESS TO RESOURCES AND TOOLS

  • Tool and Seed Distribution: Provide smallholder farmers with quality tools, seeds, and other critical inputs to enable them grow food more effectively. This empowers them to increase their productivity and become food self-sufficient.
  • Co-Operatives Support: Target cooperatives with a focus on food security, where communities can share resources and knowledge, which strengthens community resilience.

SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING

  • Capacity Building and Training: Empower communities with knowledge on sustainable farming techniques, crop diversification, and climate-smart agriculture. This ensures that they are able to grow enough food for themselves and even create wealth with surplus produce.
  • Entrepreneurship Programs: Provide training in food-related businesses, such as food processing, packaging, and preservation. This helps create local food industries and reduces reliance on external aid.

WOMEN'S DEVELOPMENT

  • Inclusivity: Ensure that, in totality, women, who are often the primary food producers and caregivers in many Nigerian communities, are not shortchanged in the empowerment initiative.
  • Leadership: Offer leadership training, access to resources, and promote efficiency in decision-making processes.

ADVOCACY

The advocacy pillar of FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is dedicated to raising awareness, influencing policies, and mobilizing communities to take action against hunger. It focuses on educating the public about the importance of food security, advocating for supportive policies, and engaging stakeholders at all levels to create a collective effort towards ending hunger in Nigeria.

CONFERENCES

  • Capacity Building: Provide a platform for capacity building, knowledge gathering and exchange of ideas. Through the various keynote, plenary and technical sessions, participants are able to share best practices, cross fertilise insights, and adopt scalable model.
  • Structured Dialogue: Promote development engagement among key actors through meaningful conversations and discussions, thus creating collaborative problem-solving approach.
  • Practical Solutions: Identify key issues, generate practical solutions through collective thinking and proffer actionable solutions that can be deployed immediately. Linking ideas with execution, concrete initiatives capable of delivering measurable impact are entrenched. Clear action plans, with timeliness, and defined roles are generated.
  • Policy Engagement: Strengthen the link between implementation realities and policy decision-making thereby producing actionable policy briefs that align with national and state development goals. Stakeholders within the public and private sectors are able to galvanize more responsive, inclusive, and effective policies that support sustainability.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

  • Local Ownership: Engage in Grassroots Mobilization and ensure that interventions are culturally appropriate, context specific and widely accepted. Through structured consultations, community leaders are involved in decision making processes thereby fostering a sense of shared responsibility. By this, FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA are driven and owned by the community.
  • Volunteers for Impact: Local residents and influencers within the communties are mobilized and trained to become advocates for food security, making them key drivers of change who bridge the gap between the program officials and beneficiaries.

YOUTH & SCHOOL OUTREACH

  • Action-Focused Programming: Engage young people through schools, secondary and tertiary, to foster a long-term commitment to fighting hunger. Interventions are delivered through structured school and youth based platforms and are aligned with national education. This includes peer education, food drives and the creation of farmers' clubs with arractive incentives.
  • Innovation: Promote youth-driven innovation by facilitating idea-generation sessions, pilot initiatives, and problem-solving activities that enable young people to develop context-specific solutions to hunger

EXHIBITION

The exhibition component of FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA will serve as a framework for stakeholders to harvest numerous strategic advantages in the food and agro-allied sector. Providing opportunities for showcasing innovative products, solutions, and technologies, the exhibition will also create a much-needed platform to receive practical contributions for ongoing improvement and customization in the quest to combat hunger and ensure food security.

KNOWLEDGE SHARING AND NETWORKING

  • Evidence Based Insights and Solutions: Bring together a diverse range of stakeholders, serving as a platform for exchange of ideas, insights and practical innovations on food solution. High-quality data, curated research, with aconable intelligence on sustainable food solutions are generated thus enabling informed leadership and decision-making capacities.
  • Strategic Alliance Formation: Provide structured networking sessions and matchmaking platforms that support the formation of long-term alliances, consorum and coalitions for sustained impact. Whilst facilitating direct engagement among stakeholders, opportunities for synergies and collaborative efforts are equally created.

PROVEN IMPACT

  • Success Stories : Highlight compelling success stories from across the country that demonstrate how intentional interventions have significantly implemented food solutions. These stories cut across various contributions by the Federal, States and Local Governments as well as communities, corporate bodies, and individuals.
  • Scalable Models: Key lessons learned from the success stories are distilled, highlighting how and why the efforts worked. Stakeholders are through this, inspired to adopt similar models and contribute to the collective efforts of fighting hunger. The pathways for scaling successful interventions are promoted, with emphasis on measurable outcomes and sustainability.

RECOGNITIONS AND AWARDS

  • Celebrating Impact, Inspiring Action: Serve as a prestigious platform to honor individuals and institutions that have demonstrated outstanding commitment, innovation, and impact in advancing food security in Nigeria. Inspiring collective commitment, and positioning excellence as catalyst for accelerated progress, the celebrations ensure that those who drive change at all levels are acknowledged and celebrated.
  • Turning Recognition into Results: Create clear award categories guided by the SDG 2 targets, and serve both as instrument of movation and accountability. The process is transparent as well as merit based guided by a structured nominaon and evaluaon framework overseen by independent panel of experts with impeccable integrity.

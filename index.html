INTRODUCTION

Food Security remains central to developmental issues across the global landscape. This is reflected in the Goal 2 of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) which addresses zero hunger. It entails the emplacements of implementation measures to ensure that everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food towards ending poverty, promoting health and fostering sustainable development.

Nigeria has experienced its own unpleasant share of food insecurity thus, forming a foundational framework for actualizing President Bola Tinubu's RENEWED HOPE AGENDA is the Enthronement of Food Security. To this end, the administration has deployed effective mechanisms to address the challenges of hunger, thus birthing the positive results currently being experienced across the country. In alignment with the administration's agenda, FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is put in place to bolster and complement the administration's goals accordingly.

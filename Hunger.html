INTRODUCTION

Food Security remains central to developmental issues across the global landscape. This is reflected in the Goal 2 of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) which addresses zero hunger. It entails the emplacements of implementation measures to ensure that everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food towards ending poverty, promoting health and fostering sustainable development.

Nigeria has experienced its own unpleasant share of food insecurity thus, forming a foundational framework for actualizing President Bola Tinubu's RENEWED HOPE AGENDA is the Enthronement of Food Security. To this end, the administration has deployed effective mechanisms to address the challenges of hunger, thus birthing the positive results currently being experienced across the country. In alignment with the administration's agenda, FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is put in place to bolster and complement the administration's goals accordingly.

THEMATIC AREAS

FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA is an initiative constructed around three interconnected thematic focus areas of Empowerment, Advocacy, and Exhibition, all designed as catalyst for sustainable growth and development. Each focus area is strategically targeted at creating solutions for hunger reduction that can be scaled and integrated across the nation's diverse population, forming a tripod towards advancing the RENEWED HOPE roadmap on Food Security in Nigeria.

EMPOWERMENT

The empowerment model of the FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA project forms a central pillar of the initiative. It focuses on equipping communities with the tools, skills, and resources necessary to become self-sufficient, improve their livelihoods, and sustainably address hunger. By focusing on education and access to resources, the program ensures that people are not only surviving hunger but are also empowered to overcome it in the long run. This approach fosters resilience, builds stronger communities, and leads to lasing changes that can transform Nigeria's food security landscape. The focus areas are as follows:

ACCESS TO RESOURCES AND TOOLS

• Tool and Seed Distribution:

Provide smallholder farmers with quality tools, improved seeds, and fertilizers, enabling them grow food more effectively. This empowers them to increase their productivity and become more self-sufficient.

• Co-Operatives Support:

Target cooperatives with a focus on food security, where communities can share resources and knowledge, strengthening their community resilience.

SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING

• Capacity Building and Training:

Empower communities with the knowledge and sustainable farming techniques in fishery, livestock, and agriculture. This ensures that they are able to grow crops and livestock, they are able to fish for themselves and generate surplus produce.

• Entrepreneurship Programs:

Provide training in food-related businesses, such as food processing, packaging, and preservation. This creates income-generating opportunities and reduces reliance on external aid.

WOMEN'S DEVELOPMENT

• Inclusivity:

Ensure that, in totality, women, who are often the primary food producers and caregivers in many Nigerian communities, are not short changed in the empowerment initiative.

• Leadership:

Offer leadership training, access to resources, and promote their voice in decision-making processes

ADVOCACY

The advocacy component of this initive is designed to mobilize actors as well as influence and shape public opinion. It will further strengthen social safety nets, enhance support for the farmers, and encourage investment in sustainable agriculture, paricularly at the subnational and local levels. It involves participatory- driven engagement tailored to the following areas:

CONFERENCES

• Capacity Building:

Provide a platform for capacity building, knowledge gathering and exchange of ideas. Through the various keynote, plenary and technical sessions, participants are able to share best practices, cross fertilise insights, and adopt scalable model.

• Structured Dialogue:

Promote development engagement among key actors through meaningful conversations and discussions, thus creating collaborative problem-solving approach.

• Practical Solutions:

Identify key issues, generate practical solutions through collective thinking and proffer actionable solutions that can be deployed immediately. Linking ideas with execution, concrete initiatives capable of delivering measurable impact are entrenched. Clear action plans, with timeliness, and defined roles are generated.

• Policy Engagement:

Strengthen the link between implementation realities and policy decision-making thereby producing actionable policy briefs that align with national and state development goals. Stakeholders within the public and private sectors are able to galvanize more responsive, inclusive, and effective policies that support sustainability.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

• Local Ownership:

Engage in Grassroots Mobilization and ensure that interventions are culturally appropriate, context specific and widely accepted. Through structured consultations, community leaders are involved in decision making processes thereby fostering a sense of shared responsibility. By this, FIGHT HUNGER NIGERIA are driven and owned by the community.

• Volunteers for Impact: :

Local residents and influencers within the communties are mobilized and trained to become advocates for food security, making them key drivers of change who bridge the gap between the program officials and beneficiaries.

YOUTH & SCHOOL OUTREACH

• Action-Focused Programming:

Engage young people through schools, secondary and tertiary, to foster a long-term commitment to fighting hunger. Interventions are delivered through structured school and youth based platforms and are aligned with national education. This includes peer education, food drives and the creation of farmers' clubs with arractive incentives.

• Innovation:

Promote youth-driven innovation by facilitating idea-generation sessions, pilot initiatives, and problem-solving activities that enable young people to develop context-specific solutions to hunger

Partnership

Our partnership objective is to foster collaborations, opportunities and benefits. Given the magnitude of Nigeria's food security issues, partnerships are at the heart of this initive. We recognize that meaningful progress in achieving the goals requires the combined efforts of all well-meaning stakeholders both at the public and private sector levels

ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

The project will adopt a structured and inclusive partnership engagement strategy designed to maximize collective impact and mutual benefits. The strategy emphasizes collaboration across government, public, corporate and private sectors ensuring complementary and results-oriented deliverables. Partners are identified through a comprehensive stakeholders mapping analysis process guided by competence and capabilities.

Corporate and Private Sector

THRIVING IN PARTNERSHIP

PARTNERSHIP CATEGORIES

Partnerships are structured to account for the various partners' respective interests across the following categories:

Platinum

Platinum partnership represents the highest level of engagement. It positions partners as strategic leaders receiving topmost-tier brand visibility across all program events and activities. This tier provides premium benefits to the partners.

Gold

Gold Partnership represents a distinguished level of participation. It provides partners with top-tier engagement and visibility across most key events and activities within program. It comes with meaningful opportunities for partners to gain insights and attracts high impact benefits

Silver

Silver Partnership offers a measurable level of engagement and visibility across selected events and activities within the program. This tier is well suited for organizations seeking involvement and visibility within specific segments of the program

Silver

This category offers an accessible entry point for public and private organizations. It establishes the organizations' willingness to align with the Renewed Hope Agenda on Food Security and the Fight Hunger Nigeria Program. It equally provides a valuable platform for visibility and lead generation.
